# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 2025**

Cameron Drury ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Idle Ant Colony mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Colony Name**: The name you choose for your ant colony (stored locally on your device)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Game Progress**: Your game data (food, ants, upgrades, achievements) is stored locally on your device
- **Device Identifiers**: Used solely for advertising purposes through Google AdMob

### Third-Party Services

#### Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device advertising identifiers
- IP address
- General location (country/region)
- App usage data for ad personalization

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

#### Apple App Store
Purchases are processed through Apple. We do not have access to your payment information. See [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
- Save and restore your game progress
- Display relevant advertisements
- Improve the app experience

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: All game data is stored locally on your device
- **No Account Required**: We do not require you to create an account
- **No Server Storage**: We do not store your personal data on external servers

## Children's Privacy

Idle Ant Colony is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

## Your Choices

### Advertising
- You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:
  - iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking
- You can purchase "Remove Ads" within the app to disable advertisements

### Data Deletion
- Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored game data
- To reset your progress, use the "New Game" option in the app

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please [open an issue](https://github.com/Cameron-Drury/idle-ant-colony-support/issues).
